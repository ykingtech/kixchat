Privacy Policy

App Name: Golden Call

Effective Date: May 11, 2025

1. Overview

Golden Call is a business phone management application operated by Lanka Knot (Pvt) Ltd, a professional caregiving services company. This app is intended for internal business use by authorised Lanka Knot staff only. It is not a consumer application open to the general public.

2. Information We Collect

a) Call Data

  • Phone numbers of incoming and outgoing calls
  • Call type (incoming, outgoing)
  • Call date, time, and duration

b) Call Notes

Entered manually by staff to maintain service records:

  • Caller name
  • Call reason (e.g. Service Booking, Caregiver Recruitment)
  • Additional notes about the interaction

c) Call Recordings

  • Audio recordings of phone calls are made automatically when a call becomes active.
  • Disclosure: Before recording begins, all parties are notified by an automated voice announcement: "This call is being recorded to provide you with a better service."
  • Recordings are stored locally on the device in the app's private storage folder.

d) Device & Technical Data

No personal device identifiers, location data, or advertising IDs are collected.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for legitimate business purposes of Lanka Knot, including:

  • Maintaining an accurate log of business calls.
  • Documenting client and caregiver interactions.
  • Improving service quality and staff accountability.
  • Resolving disputes or verifying communications.

4. Call Recording Disclosure & Consent

This app records telephone calls. All call parties are informed of recording via an automated audio announcement at the start of every call. By continuing a call after hearing the announcement, all parties consent to being recorded.

Storage: Call recordings are stored locally on the staff member's device and are not shared with third parties. They are retained only for internal business review purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: Call records and recordings are stored on the device using encrypted app-private storage. No other app can access these files.

Cloud Sync: Call log metadata (phone number, date, duration, notes) is synced to Google Firebase Firestore, hosted on secure Google Cloud infrastructure. Audio recordings are not uploaded to the cloud.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties except:

  • Google Firebase / Firestore: For cloud sync of call log data, governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law, court order, or government authority.

7. Data Retention

Call records and notes are retained for as long as necessary for business operations. Staff may delete individual records from within the app at any time. When the app is uninstalled, all locally stored data including recordings is permanently deleted.

8. Access and Rights

Since this app is for internal business use only, data access is restricted to authorised Lanka Knot staff. If you believe your call was recorded and wish to request a copy or deletion of your data, please contact us.

9. Children's Privacy

This application is not directed at or intended for use by children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
